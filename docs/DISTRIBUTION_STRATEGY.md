## üèÜ The Recommended Playbook (Limited Resources)

### Phase 1: Seeding (Weeks 1-4)
**Budget: $0-200**

1. **Identify 20-30 micro-influencers** who are:
   - Beauty professionals (not just beauty influencers)
   - Active on TikTok AND Instagram
   - 5K-50K followers

2. **Reach out with this message:**

3. **Goal:** Get 10 people actively using and creating content

### Phase 2: Organic Viral Push (Weeks 5-12)
**Budget: $0 (your time)**

1. Create your **own TikTok account** (@resultaapp)
   - Post 3-5x/week
   - Show the app in action
   - Feature user transformations (with permission)
   - Behind-the-scenes of building features

2. **Encourage UGC:**
   - Feature users who tag you
   - Create a "Creator of the Week" spotlight
   - Share/duet content made with Resulta

3. **Track watermark appearances** - When you see Resulta watermarks in the wild, engage with that content

### Phase 3: Niche Expansion (Months 3-6)
**Budget: $200-500/month**

Once you have traction with one niche (e.g., nail techs), expand to:
- Hair stylists (much larger market)
- Lash/brow artists
- Skincare/aestheticians
- Medical spas
- Tattoo artists (adjacent vertical)

## üí° The Secret Weapon: Your Watermark

Your watermark strategy is **brilliant** - but amplify it:

1. **Make the watermark a feature, not a limitation**
   - "Get discovered with the Resulta watermark - other pros will find you"
   - Position removal as a "brand establishment" upgrade

2. **Track watermark virality**
   - Set up Google Alerts for "Made with Resulta"
   - Monitor hashtags
   - This is your organic flywheel metric

3. **Consider a unique watermark per user** (future feature)
   - Would help track attribution
   - Users might WANT to keep their personalized watermark

## ‚ùå What NOT To Do

1. **Don't pay $5K+ for a single "big" influencer** - ROI is terrible at your stage
2. **Don't run Meta ads without organic validation** - You'll burn cash learning
3. **Don't spread across all platforms equally** - Focus TikTok first
4. **Don't target "general" beauty enthusiasts** - Target business owners only
5. **Don't make the app feel "corporate"** - Beauty pros want authentic tools

## üé¨ Final Recommendation

**Start with TikTok organic + micro-influencer partnerships.**

Here's why this is optimal for your situation:
1. **$0-500 budget** - Fits limited resources
2. **Your ICP lives there** - Beauty pros scroll TikTok for inspo
3. **Before/After content is NATIVE** to TikTok format
4. **Viral potential** - One good video can change everything
5. **Builds authentic relationships** - These early users become advocates

The paid influencer route only makes sense AFTER you've proven organic resonance. If a nail tech with 10K followers makes a genuine video saying "this app saves me 2 hours/week," that's worth 10x more than a paid #ad from someone with 1M followers.
